Michal Masny

I am a PhD student in philosophy at Princeton University. I completed the BPhil in Philosophy at Oxford University in 2018, under the supervision of Jeff McMahan, Tim Williamson, and Anders Sandberg. Before that, I read for the BA in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics at Warwick University.

I work on topics in normative ethics, practical ethics, and epistemology. I am also interested in metaphysics of value and decision theory.
