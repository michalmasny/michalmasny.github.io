I am a member of the Effective Altruism movement and have taken the pledge to donate at least 10% of my income over the course of my life to organizations that tackle the world's most pressing problems in the most effective ways. You can learn more about why it is important to give, which charities do the most good, and the impact your donations would make here and here.

In my spare time, I enjoy running. I train daily, and race marathons and ultramarathons. My longest event thus far has been 100 kilometres, but I also plan to do a 100-miler at some point. I also love fastpacking. Some of my more memorable trips include: tracing the coastline of Poland, crossing Iceland from north to south, and circumnavgating Mont Blanc.
